<h1 class='SubTopic'>App Lock Policy</h1>

<p class='Details'>

&nbsp &nbsp &nbsp &nbsp &nbsp
To ensure the security of your device and data, the following app lock policy is in effect:

<br>
&nbsp &nbsp &nbsp &nbsp &nbsp
AppLock allows you to secure the app every time you exit it. Once AppLock is activated, you will be prompted to enter the your set pin each time you launch the app. This feature ensures that your user data and personal information within the app remain protected and require proper authentication for access.

<br><br>

&nbsp &nbsp &nbsp &nbsp &nbsp To activate AppLock on the app, please enter the 5-digit PIN below that you can personally remember. Then, tap the 'Enable' button to activate AppLock immediately.

<br>
&nbsp &nbsp &nbsp &nbsp &nbsp To deactivate AppLock on the app, please enter the 5-digit PIN below that you used to activate AppLock. Then, tap the 'Disable' button to deactivate AppLock immediately.

<br><br>
<b>Note:<b><br>
&nbsp &nbsp &nbsp &nbsp &nbsp In case you forget your AppLock PIN, tap the 'Forgot PIN' option at the opening of the app. You will then need to enter the email you used to sign up for the account and tap 'Recover'. The PIN will be sent to your email.

</p>
